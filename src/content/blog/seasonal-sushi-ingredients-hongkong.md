Hong Kong sushi chefs follow Japan's seasonal calendar, rotating ingredients for peak flavor and freshness. Timing your visit strategically ensures the best experience.

## Spring (March-May)

**Peak Ingredients**
- **Hotaru Ika** (firefly squid) — Tender, slightly sweet
- **Kinome** — Young pepper leaves, aromatic garnish
- **Maguro** — Lean tuna, subtle flavor
- **Kohada** — Small Japanese jack, pickled, umami-rich

**Why Visit**: Delicate, refined flavors after winter

## Summer (June-August)

**Peak Ingredients**
- **Ayu** (sweetfish) — Small, fresh-caught river fish
- **Hamo** (pike conger) — Trendy, elegant
- **Uni** (sea urchin) — Hokkaido varieties peak
- **Ebi** (shrimp) — Sweet, tender varieties

**Why Visit**: Light, refreshing choices perfect for heat

## Autumn (September-November)

**Peak Ingredients**
- **Sanma** (Pacific saury) — Rich, oily, distinctive
- **Matsutake mushrooms** — Earthy garnish
- **Shad** — Seasonal delicacy
- **Aged toro** — Enhanced umami from aging

**Why Visit**: Rich, complex flavors; harvest season

## Winter (December-February)

**Peak Ingredients**
- **Buri** (yellowtail) — Fatty, buttery texture
- **Shirako** (cod milt) — Creamy, luxury ingredient
- **Tai** (sea bream) — Peak sweetness
- **Toro** — Fattiest, most indulgent

**Why Visit**: Premium, indulgent experience

## How to Maximize Seasonal Visits

**Ask Your Chef**
- "What's at peak season today?"
- "What's your seasonal recommendation?"
- "Which ingredients just arrived?"

**Menu Variations**
- High-end omakase changes daily based on market
- Kaiten rotates seasonal specials weekly
- Pricing peaks during harvest season

**Booking Strategy**
- Spring/Fall offer best value + quality
- Summer good for lighter preferences
- Winter for indulgent, rich experiences
- Book during off-seasons for shorter waits

## Sustainability Note

Eating seasonally supports sustainable fishing practices. Chef-selected seasonal menus naturally rotate stock, reducing pressure on any single species.

Time your Hong Kong sushi journey to these seasons for the most authentic, flavorful experience.
